These instructions will show you how to make an easy origami flying cicada. If you’re new to paper folding we recommend you take a minute to review the Beginner’s Guide which will teach you all the basic folds and techniques.

Step 1) Fold the paper in half.

Step 2) Fold both sides up along the dotted
lines.

Step 3) Fold both sides down along the dotted
lines.

Step 4) Fold the top layer of paper down along
the dotted line

Step 5) Fold the bottom layer of paper down
along the dotted line.

Step 6) Fold the top of the paper down along
the dotted line.

Step 7) Fold both sides out along the dotted
lines to make the wings.

Step 8) Fold both sides of the paper behind
the model.

Step 9) Turn the paper over.

Step 10) Make 3 Pleat Folds on the bottom of
the model.
